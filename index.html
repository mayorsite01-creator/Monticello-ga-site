A Clear Agenda for Monticello

The Strategic Vision

Three focused pillars — each one honoring Monticello's legacy as a Georgia City of Excellence while building a stronger, more connected, and more accessible city for every resident of Jasper County.

Heritage & Community

Pillar One

As Jasper County's seat of government since 1809 and a certified Georgia City of Excellence, Monticello carries a proud legacy. Mayor Thurman is committed to preserving this heritage while investing in the parks, events, and public spaces that bring the community together.

Economic Development

Pillar Two

Building a stronger Monticello means creating conditions where downtown businesses can grow and new employers want to invest in Jasper County. Mayor Thurman is dedicated to reducing barriers, supporting local entrepreneurs, and positioning Monticello for sustainable growth.

Transparent Governance

Pillar Three

Every Monticello resident deserves to know how their city is run. Mayor Thurman is committed to open records, accessible council minutes, clear budget reporting, and a digital office that makes city government genuinely easy to navigate for everyone.

About Mayor Thurman

Rooted in Monticello.
Committed to Its Future.
